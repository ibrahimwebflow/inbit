How Inbit Works

Simple, transparent process designed for both freelancers and clients

01

Create Your Profile

Sign up as a freelancer and complete your profile with skills, experience, and preferences.

  • Upload government ID for verification
  • Add your skills and expertise
  • Set your communication preferences
  • Choose your preferred language and tone
02

Get Skills Verified

Submit your skills for admin verification to increase your credibility and matching priority.

  • Submit proof of expertise
  • Admin reviews and verifies skills
  • Get verified badges on your profile
  • Higher weight in matching algorithm
03

Get Matched with Jobs

Our algorithm automatically matches you with relevant projects based on your verified skills.

  • Receive job matches in your dashboard
  • See match percentage and required skills
  • Review project details and budget
  • Accept projects that interest you
04

Work & Submit

Complete the project using our structured workflow with previews and final submissions.

  • Submit previews for client feedback
  • Use built-in chat for communication
  • Upload final work for approval
  • Get client sign-off
05

Get Paid Securely

Receive direct payments with our secure proof system and transparent fee structure.

  • Provide your payment details (bank/crypto)
  • Client sends payment with proof
  • Confirm payment receipt
  • Pay 5% platform fee after success
01

Create Client Account

Sign up as a client and verify your account to start posting projects.

  • Register with business or individual account
  • Upload ID for verification
  • Set communication preferences
  • Complete your company profile
02

Post Your Project

Create a detailed project description with required skills and budget.

  • Describe project requirements clearly
  • Select required skills from our database
  • Set project timeline and budget
  • Choose communication preferences
03

Review Matches

Get instantly matched with pre-vetted freelancers who fit your project needs.

  • View matched freelancers in dashboard
  • See match scores and verified skills
  • Review portfolios and past work
  • Check ratings and completed projects
04

Hire & Collaborate

Select the best freelancer and work together using our collaboration tools.

  • Hire directly from matched list
  • Use built-in chat for communication
  • Review preview submissions
  • Provide feedback and guidance
05

Pay Securely

Make payments directly to freelancers with our secure proof system.

  • Review and approve final work
  • Send payment directly to freelancer
  • Upload payment proof
  • No platform fees on your side
